About the Trivial Game

The proyect to develop in the well-known game Trivial Pusuit, but in desktop version. The aim of the game is to throw a dice, obtain a number, move the piece to the correct square, aswer a question and go on.

Also, we have to aswer correctly 1 question of each type, and the player who do it fist, will be the winner!

You can download the proyect in three different froms, to help us to imprive our work!
